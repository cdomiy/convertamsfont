How to Use AMS Fonts

1

Convert Your Text

Use our AMS Font Converter to convert Unicode text to AMS encoding. AMS fonts require AMS-encoded text to display correctly.

2

Install the Font

Download and install the AMS font on your computer. On Windows, right-click the TTF file and select "Install".

3

Apply in Your App

Open CorelDRAW, Photoshop, or any application, select the AMS font, and paste your converted text. It will render in calligraphy style.
