Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ConvertAMSFont.com ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website.

2. Text Data — We Don't Collect It

All text conversion on our website happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not send, store, or have access to any text you input or convert. Your text stays on your device at all times.

3. Information We Collect

We use third-party services that may collect limited technical information:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic patterns. This collects anonymized data such as page views, browser type, device type, and country-level location. We do not collect personally identifiable information through Analytics.
  • Google AdSense: We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

Our website itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set cookies. These are standard analytics and advertising cookies used across the web.

5. Third-Party Services

Our transliteration feature uses Google Input Tools API to suggest Devanagari text as you type in English. When you use this feature, your English keystrokes are sent to Google's servers for transliteration. This is optional and can be turned off using the "Off" button in the transliteration bar.

6. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS, ensuring encrypted communication. Since we don't collect or store your text data, there is no risk of text data breach on our end.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our About page for contact information.
